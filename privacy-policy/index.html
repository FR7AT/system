Privacy Policy for OP System

Your privacy is important to us. Please review our policy below.

1. Introduction

Welcome to the OP System Discord Bot Privacy Policy. This document explains how we handle your data when you use our bot. By using OP System, you agree to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use OP System, we may collect the following information:

  • User IDs: To identify users and maintain logs for bot operations.
  • Server IDs: To provide server-specific configurations and features.
  • Channel IDs: For managing channel-specific commands and settings.
  • Command Inputs: Temporarily stored for processing and execution, but not saved beyond operation.

No sensitive personal information, such as emails or passwords, is collected or stored.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Deliver the bot's functionalities (e.g., executing commands, sending automated messages).
  • Maintain bot performance and security.
  • Provide server-specific configurations.
  • Improve user experience and develop new features.

4. Data Retention

OP System retains data only for as long as necessary to provide its services. Temporary data (e.g., command inputs) is deleted immediately after processing. Logs for troubleshooting and debugging may be stored temporarily and are regularly purged.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

Your data will never be sold or shared with third parties. We may disclose information only under the following circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations or government requests.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • To request information about the data we collect and process.
  • To request data deletion if applicable.
  • To opt out of certain features or functionalities that require data collection.

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the channels below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be notified via official channels or the bot's information page. Your continued use of OP System after updates signifies acceptance of the revised policy.
